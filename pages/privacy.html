Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At uPlayGammz, your privacy and the security of your personal data are our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our gaming tournament platform.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide you with a seamless gaming experience and comply with legal financial regulations. This includes:

  • Profile Data: First name, last name, username, email address, and WhatsApp number.
  • Gaming Data: Linked platform IDs (e.g., PSN, Xbox Live, Steam) and tournament match results.
  • Financial Data: Bank account numbers and names solely for the purpose of processing secure tournament payouts (verified via secure third-party APIs).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is utilized strictly for platform operations:

  • To verify your identity and ensure fair play in competitive matches.
  • To securely process wallet deposits, entry fees, and prize withdrawals.
  • To communicate important updates regarding tournaments, matches, and account security.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data to advertisers. We only share necessary data with trusted third-party partners (such as secure payment gateways like Paystack) strictly for verifying bank details and processing financial transactions.

4. Security Measures

uPlayGammz employs industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your data. Your financial details are never stored in plain text, and we highly recommend enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) in your Account Settings for maximum security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or permanently delete your personal information at any time via the Account Settings panel. If you request account deletion, all personal data will be purged from our active databases in accordance with local regulations.
